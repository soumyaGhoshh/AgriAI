Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Krishi Bandhu

Is Krishi Bandhu free to use?

Yes! All core features including crop disease detection, weather forecasts, and farming advisories are completely free for farmers. We may introduce premium features in the future with nominal charges.

Do I need to create an account?

While you can access basic features without an account, creating a free account allows you to save your farm locations, track detection history, and receive personalized recommendations.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We follow strict data protection protocols and never share your personal information or farm data with third parties without your consent.

How accurate is the disease detection?

Our AI model achieves 90-95% accuracy for common crop diseases when provided with clear images of affected leaves. Accuracy may vary for less common diseases or poor quality images.

What crops does the system recognize?

We currently support major crops including rice, wheat, maize, cotton, sugarcane, and various vegetables. We're continuously adding support for more crops.

How should I take photos for best results?

- Take photos in natural daylight
- Focus on affected leaves (fill 70% of frame)
- Include both healthy and affected parts
- Avoid shadows and blurry images
- Take multiple angles if possible

What if the detection seems incorrect?

Our system provides multiple possible matches with confidence percentages. If uncertain, you can submit the image for expert review (available in premium) or try taking clearer photos from different angles.

How current is the weather data?

We update weather information every 30 minutes from trusted meteorological sources including IMD. Our forecasts are typically accurate for 3-5 days.

How are farming advisories generated?

Our AI analyzes weather patterns, soil conditions (if provided), crop stage, and regional farming practices to generate personalized recommendations.

Can I get alerts for weather changes?

Yes! After creating an account, you can enable SMS/email notifications for critical weather events like heavy rain, frost, or heat waves.

What browsers are supported?

Krishi Bandhu works best on Chrome, Firefox, and Edge. For mobile, also you can use the webiste. It is properly responsive.

The page isn't loading properly - what should I do?

1. Refresh the page
2. Clear your browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection
If problems persist, contact our support team.
